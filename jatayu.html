About Jatayu

The Jatayu Earth Center in Kerala is home to the world's largest bird sculpture — a tribute to the mythological bird Jatayu. Surrounded by lush hills, it offers adventure activities, cable car rides, and panoramic views of nature.

What I Experienced

It felt surreal standing beneath the massive sculpture, feeling the mythology come alive. The cable car ride offered breathtaking views, and the entire atmosphere had a sense of peace mixed with thrill. Definitely a must-visit in Kerala!
