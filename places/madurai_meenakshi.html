📖 My Story

The Meenakshi Temple is one of the most iconic temples I visited in Tamil Nadu. The intricate architecture, colorful gopurams, and spiritual vibes made it unforgettable. I spent the day exploring the inner sanctum and walking around the bustling market outside.
